Biography

I am an independent Principal Investigator (PI) at the Data Management Innovation Research Center, Nanjing University. My research expertise lies in Science of Science, Complex Networks, and Scientometrics. I am particularly interested in the career development paths of young scholars and the mechanisms of academic mentorship.

My recent research, published in Nature Human Behaviour, explores how academic group size influences mentee success. This work has been highlighted by Nature News.
